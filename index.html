Bootcamp Final Project · Business Analysis

EnterpriseGoose Expansion Analysis

Analysed five years of consulting firm data across clients, projects, consultants, services and leads to generate business insights and produce data-backed expansion recommendations for a firm planning significant growth over the next 12 months.

UK and Ireland represent 71.7% of the total client base (171 and 80 clients respectively) — high concentration posing significant business risk if either market declines
France and Germany generate the most leads per existing client — strongest organic growth signal, identified as priority expansion markets
Kenya has the highest lead conversion rate at 50% — the strongest of any market analysed, indicating a significant untapped growth opportunity
Climate & Sustainability leads industry conversion rates at 50% from 10 leads — recommended focus for international outreach alongside Technology
Total revenue 2021–2026: €505,879 — with 2025 the highest earning year at €246,605 across 167 projects; top 6 consultants contributed €3.1M of total project revenue
Recommendation: expand aggressively into France, Germany and Kenya targeting Climate & Sustainability and Technology clients, where conversion potential is highest
